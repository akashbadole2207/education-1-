About:

Welcome to Bright Tutorial, Wardha

Bright Tutorial is a pioneer coaching centre of Wardha. Our classes were set up from 2015 with an aim to provide coaching to the aspiring students who are dedicated and want to achieve excellence in their career. We possess unmatched record of providing coaching to numerous students and they are excelling in their lives. Our institution is appreciated by students unique and practical base teaching technique for making the process of learning simpler and easier. We have a team of highly qualified and skilled teachers who are serving their domain for years. At our institution, we have all the requisite facilities that are needed to render high standard education. In addition to this, comprehensive study material is provided to each student that is thoroughly preparing by our qualified teachers keeping in mind the current exam patterns.

Vision

a) Our basic motto is to constantly improve and strengthen the positive impact we have on our student and society, helping them in transforming their lives through our dedication and hard work. This encourages us to make a difference to the society.
b) To empower students to built stronger concepts to make them more confident to face any academic challenges in the future.

Mission

To help student to achieve their academic objectives by providing innovation, best in class coaching through interactive technology and methodology and to make learning meaningful and joyful.
